Acne is a skin condition caused by clogged pores and bacteria.

Symptoms include pimples, blackheads, and whiteheads.

Lifestyle changes such as washing the face regularly and avoiding oily foods can help reduce acne.

Eczema causes itchy and inflamed skin.

Moisturizing the skin frequently helps manage eczema.

Psoriasis is an autoimmune condition causing rapid skin cell growth.

Stress reduction and proper skincare help control psoriasis.
